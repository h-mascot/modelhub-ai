# What Happens When AI Model Quality Converges?

When model quality converges, buying behavior changes.
The winner is no longer the provider with the best demo. It is the product that makes access, routing, and workflow easiest.

## What becomes more important

### Distribution
Users pick what they encounter and can adopt quickly.

### Workflow
The easiest product to use repeatedly gains an advantage.

### Pricing simplicity
Clearer pricing becomes a feature in a crowded market.

### Aggregation
If several models are good enough, the ability to move across them becomes more valuable than allegiance to one.

## Final takeaway

Convergence shifts value upward from raw model capability to product experience. That is good news for aggregator businesses with strong UX and clear economics.
