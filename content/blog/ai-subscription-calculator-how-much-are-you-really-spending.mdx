# AI Subscription Calculator: How Much Are You Really Spending on AI?

When someone asks how much they spend on AI, they usually name one subscription. "$20 a month for ChatGPT." But that number is almost always wrong. Here is how to find your real AI spend.

## The categories most people forget

### Category 1: Direct AI subscriptions
The obvious ones:
- ChatGPT Plus ($20/month)
- Claude Pro ($20/month)
- Gemini Advanced ($20/month)
- Midjourney ($10-60/month)
- Perplexity Pro ($20/month)

### Category 2: AI features in tools you already pay for
These add AI costs to existing subscriptions:
- Notion AI ($10/user/month add-on)
- GitHub Copilot ($10-39/user/month)
- Slack AI (varies by plan)
- Zoom AI Companion (varies by plan)
- Microsoft 365 Copilot ($30/user/month)
- Adobe Firefly (included in Creative Cloud, but CC costs $55+/month)

### Category 3: API usage
Pay-per-use that fluctuates:
- OpenAI API usage
- Anthropic API usage
- Google AI Studio usage
- Stability AI or other image generation APIs

### Category 4: Embedded AI tools
Standalone products powered by AI:
- Jasper ($49-99/month)
- Copy.ai ($49+/month)
- Grammarly Premium ($12/month)
- Otter.ai ($10-20/month)
- Descript ($24-33/month)

### Category 5: Team sprawl
Individual subscriptions that should be team plans:
- 5 people each paying $20/month = $100/month
- A team plan might cost $40/month

## Common spending patterns

**The Unconscious Spender** — Has 3-4 AI subscriptions but only uses 2 regularly. Total: $60-80/month but $40-60 is wasted.

**The Tool Collector** — Tries every new AI tool, subscribes to 5+, forgets to cancel. Total: $100-200/month with 40% redundancy.

**The Power User** — Uses 2-3 tools intensively, pays for API access, and has embedded AI in workflow tools. Total: $80-150/month, mostly efficient.

**The Team Manager** — Overseeing team subscriptions, some duplicated, some individual where team plans would save money. Total: $200-500/month for a 10-person team.

## How to audit your AI spending

1. Open your bank/credit card statements for the last 3 months
2. Search for: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google AI, Midjourney, Jasper, Notion, Grammarly, Copilot
3. Check each tool's billing page for API usage charges
4. Ask your team what AI tools they use (you will find subscriptions you did not know about)
5. Calculate your total

## The hidden cost of fragmentation

It is not just money. Multiple subscriptions mean:
- Time switching between tools (estimated 10-15 min/day for heavy users)
- Prompts and context scattered across platforms
- No consolidated view of AI usage or ROI
- Onboarding friction for new team members
