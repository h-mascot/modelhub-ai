# How to Reduce AI Spend Without Reducing Output

Most teams cut AI spend the wrong way.
They cancel a tool and lose capability.
A better approach is to remove waste while keeping output quality high.

## Where waste usually hides

- duplicate subscriptions
- backup tools nobody uses consistently
- one-off experiments that became recurring charges
- poor routing that sends every task to the most expensive option

## How to cut spend intelligently

### Consolidate access
If the team already needs multiple models, move to a single aggregator workspace where possible.

### Route by task complexity
Not every task deserves a premium model.

### Audit monthly
Look at actual use, not narrative.

### Standardize workflows
When teams know which model to use for which task, waste drops.

## Final takeaway

The goal is not fewer models. It is fewer unnecessary payments and fewer chaotic habits.
