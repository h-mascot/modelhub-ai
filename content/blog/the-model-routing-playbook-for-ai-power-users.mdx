# The Model Routing Playbook for AI Power Users

Power users stop asking which model is best and start asking which model is best for this prompt right now.
That shift is model routing.

## Routing by task

### Use writing-first models for tone and narrative

### Use structure-first models for implementation and decomposition

### Use context-heavy models for research and large inputs

## Routing by cost

Not every task deserves your most expensive model.
Cheap tasks should stay cheap.
Complex tasks should earn the premium.

## Why aggregators matter

Routing is annoying across separate apps and easy inside one shared workspace.
That is why aggregator products become sticky with experienced users.

## Final takeaway

Model routing is what mature AI usage looks like. The users who master it get both better outputs and cleaner economics.
