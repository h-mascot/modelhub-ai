# Build vs Buy Your Company AI Stack

Every company with technical ambition eventually asks whether it should build its own AI stack.
Usually the honest answer is: not yet.

## When buying is smarter

Buy when the main need is access, speed, and team adoption.
Most teams do not need to reinvent routing, billing, and multi-model access before they have real usage maturity.

## When building is smarter

Build when AI is core to the product, workflow complexity is high, and you have clear requirements direct tools cannot meet.

## Practical middle ground

Many companies should buy for human workflows and build only where product differentiation requires it.

## Final takeaway

If your team is still figuring out how to use AI consistently, buy the operating system first. Build later if the workflow proves it deserves custom infrastructure.
