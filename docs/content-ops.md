# ModelHub AI Content Ops

## Goal
Turn blog traffic into free-trial starts by pairing high-intent SEO articles with measurable conversion paths.

## Content clusters
- Cost comparisons
- LLM routing playbooks
- Team onboarding and procurement
- Role-specific tutorials

## Conversion path
1. Article view
2. Article CTA click
3. Chat landing with attribution params
4. Pricing click or product usage

## Analytics events now instrumented
- `article_view`
- `article_open`
- `article_cta_click`
- `signup_landing`
- `newsletter_start`
- `newsletter_submit`
- `cta_click`
- `pricing_click`

## Weekly review
Track these by article slug:
- Pageviews
- CTA click-through rate
- Chat landings
- Pricing clicks
- Trial-to-paid notes
